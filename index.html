美国家庭的四道防线体系

Prevention Over Reaction

预防胜过反应

Criminals often target homes where they see signs of inattentiveness—packages left out, unlocked doors, poor lighting.

犯罪分子经常以那些显示疏于防范的住宅为目标——包裹外放、门未上锁、照明不足。

Being aware of surroundings, routines, and unusual activity makes it easier to spot potential threats before they escalate.

对周围环境、日常例行公事和异常活动保持警觉，有助于在潜在威胁升级之前发现它们。
Scenario: Preventing incidents is part of being a responsible home owner, being aware of your homes surroundings at any time allows you to assess and quickly adapt to any situation.
情景：预防事故是负责任房主的一部分，随时了解家庭周围环境可以让您评估并快速适应任何情况。

Situational Awareness

情境感知

This is an inherit advantage that people use in their daily lives, some better than others. .

这是人们在日常生活中使用的固有优势，有些人比其他人做得更好。

Coming home from being away is when you may notice something is unuasual, which could warrant an investigation.

外出回家时可能会注意到异常情况，这可能需要调查。
Scenario: You come home and see the door is slightly open, or a light is on that previously wasn't, these types of indicators can cause someone to be "on edge" until a conclusion is made.
情景：您回家看到门微微开着，或者之前没有开的灯亮着，这些指标会让人"紧张"，直到得出结论。

Faster Response Time

更快响应时间

Awareness means noticing strange vehicles, unfamiliar people, or odd noises right away.

警觉意味着立即注意到陌生车辆、不熟悉的人或奇怪的声音。

Quick recognition allows for immediate action—calling police, triggering alarms, or locking doors.

快速识别能够立即采取行动——报警、触发警报或锁门。
Scenario: Someone sitting outside in their car for an extended amount of time can cause concern, some people may even notify the police.
情景：有人在车里长时间坐在外面会引起担忧，有些人甚至可能通知警察。

Visible & Audible Deterrence Effect

可见和听觉威慑效果

A household that's visibly alert—lights on timers, dog barking, cameras positioned well, signs, people checking surroundings—signals to would-be intruders that it's not an easy target.

一个明显警觉的家庭——定时器控制的灯光、狗叫声、摄像头位置良好、标志、人们检查周围环境——向潜在入侵者发出信号，表明这不是一个容易的目标。

Criminals are more likely to bypass homes where residents seem security-conscious.

犯罪分子更可能绕过那些居民看起来有安全意识的住宅。
Scenario: Most criminals will avoid homes that "appear" more secure with these added measures, some homeowner may even have a sign "gun on premise".
情景：大多数罪犯会避开那些通过这些额外措施"看起来"更安全的房屋，一些房主甚至可能有"房内有枪"的标志。

Protection of Family & Community

保护家庭和社区

Awareness isn't just about the home—it extends to children's play areas, neighbors' homes, and community safety.

警觉不仅仅关于家庭——它延伸到儿童游乐区、邻居家和社区安全。

A vigilant neighborhood can reduce crime rates and create a sense of shared responsibility.

一个警觉的社区可以降低犯罪率并创造共同责任感。
Scenario: Informing your neighbors is a way to keep aware of what has been happening nearby, some neighbors will be more vigilant than others and may feel a sense of responsibility to "watch over" suspicious activities. "Neighborhood Watches" are relatively common.
情景：通知邻居是了解附近发生的事情的一种方式，一些邻居会比其他邻居更警觉，可能会感到有责任"监视"可疑活动。"邻里守望"相对常见。

Adapting to Modern Risks

适应现代风险

Threats today aren't just break-ins—they include porch piracy, car theft, and scams.

今天的威胁不仅仅是入室盗窃——还包括门廊盗窃、汽车盗窃和诈骗。

Awareness helps people adapt their habits, use smart tech effectively, and protect deliveries, vehicles, and personal information.

警觉有助于人们调整习惯、有效使用智能技术，并保护快递、车辆和个人信息。
Scenario: Depending on the income level, knowing what latest tech is available is appealing so that you can optimize your own home to adapt to a dynamcally changing neighborhood.
情景：根据收入水平，了解最新可用技术是有吸引力的，这样您就可以优化自己的家庭以适应动态变化的社区。

2nd Line - NOTIFICATION

第二道防线 - 通知预警

Activity alerts, motion detection, smart notifications

活动警报、动态检测、智能通知

Immediate Threat Awareness

即时威胁意识

Notifications bridge the gap between an event happening and the homeowner knowing about it.

通知弥合了安全事件发生与房主了解之间的差距。

Even if the first layer (awareness) is missed, alerts instantly draw attention to movement, unusual activity, or a triggered sensor.

即使第一层（感知）被遗漏，警报也会立即将注意力吸引到运动、异常活动或触发的传感器上。
Scenario: "You can't be everywhere at once" is a common English saying. But if something unusual outside your property is happenining, you want to know what it is. A dog bark to a motion light activating, you become instantly curious what triggered it.
情景："你不能同时出现在所有地方"是一个常见的英语说法。但如果你的房产外发生了不寻常的事情，你想知道那是什么。从狗叫到运动灯激活，你会立即好奇是什么触发了它。

Action Without Delay

立即行动

A push alert or siren cue lets the homeowner respond right away—whether that means speaking through a camera, turning on lights, or calling authorities.

推送警报或警报器提示让房主可以立即响应——无论是通过摄像头说话、开灯还是报警。

This quick reaction can stop an incident before it escalates.

这种快速反应可以在事件升级之前阻止它。
Scenario: There is a sense of responsibility for you to take action on situations around your home, for an average person, an alert requires personal investigation, and a response. From raccoons in the trash to someone breaking into your car, you rely on knowing in order to take action.
情景：你有责任对家周围的情况采取行动，对于普通人来说，警报需要个人调查和响应。从浣熊翻垃圾到有人破坏你的车，你依靠了解情况才能采取行动。

Extending Protection Beyond the Home

将保护延伸到家庭之外

Notifications mean you're still "home" even when you're away.

通知意味着即使您不在家，您仍然"在家"。

Whether you're at work, on vacation, or just in the backyard, alerts keep you connected to what's happening at your property.

无论您在工作、度假还是只是在后院，警报都让您与您的财产发生的事情保持联系。
Scenario: Going to the store, a resturant, visiting a family or friends home, going on vacation there are multiple instances when you are not home, you might be waiting for a package delivery or possibly someone to check your home, you still want to know what's going one around your personal belongings.
情景：去商店、餐厅、拜访家人或朋友的家、度假，有很多时候你不在家，你可能在等包裹递送或可能有人来检查你的家，你仍然想知道你的个人物品周围发生了什么。

Customized to What Matters

定制重要事项

Smart notifications filter out irrelevant motion, reducing false alarms.

智能通知过滤掉无关的运动，减少误报。

This ensures that when you do get an alert, it's something worth checking—helping maintain trust in the system.

这确保当您收到警报时，这是值得检查的事情——有助于维持对系统的信任。

NOTE: Every security set up is "custom" to a persons needs and home, especially for DIY.

注意：每个安全设置都是根据个人需求和家庭"定制"的，特别是对于DIY。
Scenario: "The Boy Who Cried Wolf" is another common saying, if a measure of security is constantly misinforming it will be disregarded. Some people even have issues with their dogs being too senstive which can be annoying.
情景："狼来了"是另一个常见的说法，如果安全措施不断提供错误信息，它将被忽视。有些人甚至遇到他们的狗太敏感的问题，这可能很烦人。

Layered Security Confidence

分层安全信心

Awareness is the first guardrail, notifications are the second.

感知是第一道护栏，通知是第二道。

Together, they create a fail-safe: if you don't see something in person, you rely on your security setup to tell you instantly.

它们一起创造了一个故障安全机制：如果您亲自没有看到某些东西，您依靠安全系统立即告诉您。
Scenario: The dog is part of your family, if it is acting weird or barking, this is a strong indicator that something is wrong for most people.
情景：狗是你家庭的一部分，如果它行为异常或吠叫，对大多数人来说这是出了问题的强烈信号。

3rd Line - INTRUSION

第三道防线 - 入侵检测

Crime detection, deterrence, recording, evidence capture

犯罪检测、威慑、录像、证据收集

The Last Barrier When Prevention Fails

预防失败时的最后屏障

Even with strong awareness and timely notifications, some threats make it to the intrusion stage.

即使有强烈的意识和及时的通知，一些威胁仍会达到入侵阶段。

This layer ensures that if a crime attempt happens, it is immediately recognized, challenged, and documented.

这一层确保如果发生犯罪企图，它会立即被识别、挑战和记录。
Scenario: A car being broken into, mischevious neighbors, dangerous animals, a package stolen, a robbery, or vandalism, these can be a major violation for people and it can even be traumatic for most, this is why the first 2 lines of defense are valued so highly as a homeowner.
情景：汽车被破坏、恶作剧邻居、危险动物、包裹被盗、抢劫或破坏行为，这些对人们来说可能是重大侵犯，甚至对大多数人来说可能是创伤性的，这就是为什么作为房主如此重视前两道防线的原因。

Real-Time Crime Detection

实时犯罪检测

Smart cameras and sensors instantly spot break-ins, trespassing, or vandalism in progress.

智能摄像头和传感器立即发现正在进行的入室盗窃、擅自进入或破坏行为。

The faster the system detects, the faster it can trigger deterrents or summon help.

系统检测得越快，就能越快触发威慑或召唤帮助。
Scenario: Robberies can happen at any moment, day or night, the logic is if you can react faster to the situation there's a better chance to solve it.
情景：抢劫可能在任何时候发生，白天或黑夜，逻辑是如果你能更快地对情况做出反应，就有更好的机会解决它。

Powerful Deterrence in the Moment

关键时刻的强大威慑

Bright floodlights, loud sirens, and live voice warnings can stop an intruder before the crime is completed.

明亮的泛光灯、响亮的警报器和实时语音警告可以在犯罪完成之前阻止入侵者。

Criminals prefer easy targets—visible, active defenses can drive them away mid-attempt.

犯罪分子偏爱容易的目标——可见的、主动的防御可以在企图中途将他们赶走。
Scenario: Catching someone in the act or "red-handed" is more likely to cause them to panic and flee, anything that may draw attention to them can potentially prevent harm, this is also a common tactic for female self-defense is to cause a "scene" to disrupt someone dangerous.
情景：抓住某人"当场"或"现行"更可能导致他们恐慌和逃跑，任何可能引起对他们注意的事情都可能防止伤害，这也是女性自卫的常见策略，即制造"场面"来扰乱危险人员。

Full Recording for Accountability

完整记录以确保问责

High-definition video and audio ensure that no detail is lost, from the suspect's appearance to the getaway vehicle.

高清视频和音频确保不会丢失任何细节，从嫌疑人的外观到逃跑车辆。

Continuous or event-triggered recording builds an indisputable timeline of the incident.

连续或事件触发的录制建立了事件的无可争议的时间线。
Scenario: Hope is in the evidence after an intrusion, if an expected camera fails during this time it can lead to a feeling of hopelessness. If a car is stolen but your camera missed it, you now have more questions than answers.
情景：入侵后希望在于证据，如果预期的摄像头在这个时候失败，可能会导致绝望感。如果汽车被盗但你的摄像头错过了，你现在的问题比答案更多。

Evidence That Holds Up

有效的证据

Clear, well-documented footage is critical for police investigations and insurance claims.

清晰、记录完善的录像对警方调查和保险索赔至关重要。

Strong evidence increases arrest rates and helps communities remove repeat offenders.

有力的证据提高逮捕率，并帮助社区清除重复犯罪者。
Scenario: Calling the police is a reasonable reaction for when you feel wronged, you want justice but also you want what happened to not happen again or to anyone else. People may go to extreme lengths to seek justice in US, involving the law is common practice.
情景：当你感到被冤枉时，报警是合理的反应，你想要正义，但你也希望发生的事情不再发生或不发生在其他人身上。在美国，人们可能会竭尽全力寻求正义，涉及法律是常见做法。

Last Line - ACTION

最后防线 - 主动防御

Emergency response, physical defense, intervention

紧急响应、物理防御、干预行动

Protection When All Else Fails

当一切都失败时的保护

If awareness, notifications, and intrusion deterrents don't stop the threat, this layer ensures there is still a way to safeguard life and property.

如果感知、通知和入侵威慑都无法阻止威胁，这一层确保仍有方法保护生命和财产。

It focuses on immediate action, containment, and recovery.

它专注于立即行动、遏制和恢复。
Scenario: Time is crucial and you and your families safety is always priority. The right to defend yourself and home is a very normal expectation in the US.
情景：时间至关重要，你和你家人的安全总是优先考虑的。在美国，保卫自己和家园的权利是一个非常正常的期望。

Emergency Response Activation

紧急响应激活

Direct connection to emergency services (police, fire, medical) for rapid dispatch.

直接连接紧急服务（警察、消防、医疗）以快速派遣。

Calling 911 or automated alerts can send critical details—location, type of incident, and live video—to responders is desirable.

拨打911或自动警报可以向响应者发送关键细节——位置、事件类型和实时视频——这是理想的。
Scenario: Calling 911 is reserved for emergencies, so you learn from a young age that 911 is very special, police response in most areas to an "active" situation is expected among Americans if that call is made. The dispatch may even stay on the phone with you and guide you until the police arrive.
情景：拨打911是为紧急情况保留的，所以你从小就知道911非常特殊，如果拨打了这个电话，美国人期望大多数地区的警察对"活跃"情况做出响应。调度员甚至可能会一直与你通话并指导你直到警察到达。

Secure Containment

安全遏制

Locks, reinforced doors/windows, and safe zones keep family members protected until help arrives.

锁具、加固的门窗和安全区域在帮助到达之前保护家庭成员。

Can include panic buttons or app-based emergency triggers.

可以包括紧急按钮或基于应用程序的紧急触发器。
Scenario: Relying on preventiative measures you can become more reliant on them, such as an anti-kick door or bars on windows, and locks on other doors in the house. This can give you time to prepare you and your family to get away, grab weapons, and call for help.
情景：依靠预防措施你可以变得更加依赖它们，比如防踢门或窗户上的栅栏，以及房子里其他门上的锁。这可以给你时间准备你和你的家人逃脱、抓取武器并呼救。

Survival & Safety First

生存和安全第一

Prioritizes getting family members to safety, whether through evacuation plans, safe rooms, or guided instructions via security systems.

优先考虑让家庭成员安全，无论是通过疏散计划、安全房间还是通过安全系统的指导指令。

Even if property loss occurs, human safety is preserved.

即使发生财产损失，人身安全也得到保护。
Scenario: It's common for homes to have multiple rooms and floors, and most people know where are the safest locations in their homes. If you are a gun owner, you may even have an action plan in place for your family.

NOTE: A gun is meant to kill, an intrusion while you are home can put your life at risk, so a gun is acceptable to defend with (In most situations.)

情景：房屋通常有多个房间和楼层，大多数人知道他们家中最安全的位置在哪里。如果你是枪支拥有者，你甚至可能为你的家人制定了行动计划。注意：枪支是用来杀戮的，当你在家时的入侵可能会危及你的生命，所以用枪防卫是可以接受的（在大多数情况下）。

Recovery & Resolution

恢复和解决

Post-incident support: insurance documentation, law enforcement coordination, and system analysis to prevent repeat incidents.

事后支持：保险文件、执法协调和系统分析以防止重复事件。

Ensures that the event is fully resolved and lessons are integrated into the home's security strategy.

确保事件得到完全解决，并将经验教训整合到家庭安全战略中。
Scenario: If you have entry into your home, afterwards you will want evidence to seek justice and find the truth. If a firearm or violence was involved, it can be used to protect you from false allegations.

NOTE: If you fire a gun in a residential area, it will always be investigated and has high potential to be taken to court.

情景：如果有人进入你的家，之后你会想要证据来寻求正义和寻找真相。如果涉及火器或暴力，它可以用来保护你免受虚假指控。注意：如果你在住宅区开枪，总是会被调查并且很有可能被起诉。

Layered Home Intrusion Deterrence & Defense

分层家庭入侵威慑和防御

Outer Perimeter – Deterrence Before Approach

外围周边 - 接近前威慑

Fences, neighborhood watch, decoys, and warning signs

围栏、社区监督、诱饵和警告标志

Fences & Gates

围栏和大门

Tall fences, locked gates, and privacy barriers seperate your home from the street, sidewalks and wildlife, while making entry harder and signal protection.

高大的围栏、上锁的大门和隐私屏障将您的家与街道、人行道和野生动物分开，同时使进入更加困难并发出保护信号。
Scenario: Someone jumping over your fence is highly suspicious, there is not many instances when this is acceptable. Also, an intruder wants to be quick, a fence can also prevent a "quick escape".
情景：有人跳过你的围栏是非常可疑的，很少有情况下这是可以接受的。而且，入侵者想要快速行动，围栏也可以阻止"快速逃脱"。

Lights On

开灯

Lights on in the evening or while you're away can signal to criminals that this house may have someone inside, which they may avoid since robbers want to be unnoticed and not have to deal with a person.

晚上或您不在时开灯可以向罪犯发出信号，表明这所房子里可能有人，他们可能会避开，因为强盗希望不被注意到，不想与人打交道。
Scenario: Especially alone, some people may turn on multiple lights in the evening to signal to people outside there are "people home" and this house is active. Front doors tend to have "lights" you can also turn on that signal someone is home and that your "available" if someone were to approach.
情景：特别是独自一人时，有些人可能在晚上开多盏灯来向外面的人发出信号，表明"家里有人"，这所房子是活跃的。前门通常有"灯"，您也可以打开，表示有人在家，如果有人接近，您是"有空的"。

Decoys & Signs

诱饵和标志

Security company signs, fake cameras, and "beware of dog" notices create perceived risk.

安全公司标志、假摄像头和"小心恶犬"通知创造了感知风险。
Scenario: People often place a sign in their yard to indicate they have a security measure in place. Some people may even buy the sign just to "deter" a criminal. Multiple of these signs in a neighborhood also can indicate a security aware environment, which criminals may avoid.
情景：人们经常在院子里放置标志来表明他们有安全措施。有些人甚至可能只是为了"威慑"罪犯而购买标志。社区中多个这样的标志也可以表明一个有安全意识的环境，罪犯可能会避开。

Neighborhood Watch & Community Alerts

社区监督和社区警报

Neighbors monitor suspicious activity and share alerts via apps or meetings. A "close community" does a lot to help each other.

邻居监视可疑活动并通过应用程序或会议分享警报。"紧密的社区"会做很多事情来互相帮助。
Scenario: From a delivery to someone near your car or on your property, if a neighbor sees it, you will likely be notified by them. A well trusted neighbor may even have a key to your home for "just-in-case" situations.
情景：从快递到有人在您的车附近或您的财产上，如果邻居看到了，他们很可能会通知您。一个值得信赖的邻居甚至可能有您家的钥匙，以备"以防万一"的情况。

Property Perimeter – Early Warning & Illumination

物业周边 - 早期预警和照明

Motion lighting, sensors, and animal deterrents

运动照明、传感器和动物威慑

Motion-Activated Lighting

运动激活照明

Floodlights activate on movement, startling and exposing people, animals and intruders. Can also signal to homeowner of event.

泛光灯在检测到运动时激活，惊吓并暴露人员、动物和入侵者。也可以向房主发出事件信号。
Scenario: Sitting at home at night and a backyard floodlight turns on randomly, this is going to raise anxiety until you figure out what caused it. It can also be useful if you need to go outside at night to easily light a "dark" are with no effort.
情景：晚上坐在家里，后院的泛光灯随机打开，这会引起焦虑，直到您找出原因。如果您需要在夜间外出，它也很有用，可以毫不费力地照亮"黑暗"区域。

Driveway & Entry Sensors

车道和入口传感器

Detect vehicles or people entering property before reaching the home. Hopefully notifying the owner of an event.

在到达房屋之前检测进入物业的车辆或人员。希望能通知业主有事件发生。
Scenario: In the basement, bedroom, or office you usually don't have a means to "see". If you get a notification, you can proactively investigate the cause rather than being surprised.
情景：在地下室、卧室或办公室，您通常没有"看到"的手段。如果您收到通知，您可以主动调查原因，而不是被吓到。

Sound

声音

Residential neighborhoods can often times be quiet, hearing a noise like a car door closing, talking, or even walking can grab a homeowners attention, especially if they are alone.

住宅区通常很安静，听到汽车门关闭、说话甚至走路等声音可以抓住房主的注意力，特别是如果他们独自一人。
Scenario: If you're in bed at night and hear a car door closing at 3am in your driveway, you might want to check it out.
情景：如果您晚上在床上，凌晨3点听到车道上有车门关闭的声音，您可能想去查看一下。

Dogs

犬类

Barking serves as both alarm and deterrent, warning owners and neighbors of a potential event.

吠叫既是警报又是威慑，警告主人和邻居潜在事件。
Scenario: If a property has a large dog, barking or being in the yard can easily deter a criminal because every dog reacts differently, There is "no" standard. A small dog can bark to alert a homeowner that someone is approaching, or signal to you there is a problem. Dogs are known as "man's best friend".
情景：如果房产有大狗，吠叫或在院子里可以轻易威慑罪犯，因为每只狗的反应都不同，没有"标准"。小狗可以吠叫来提醒房主有人接近，或向您发出有问题的信号。狗被称为"人类最好的朋友"。

Home Exterior – Physical Barriers & Surveillance

房屋外部 - 物理屏障和监控

Cameras, reinforced entry points, and security films

摄像头、加固入口点和安全薄膜

Security Cameras

安全摄像头

Provide live monitoring, alerts, and recorded evidence. This is useful for daily activities, while away, and keeping an eye on vulnerable areas while providing a relief making sure everything is "OK".

提供实时监控、警报和录制证据。这对日常活动、外出时以及监视脆弱区域都很有用，同时提供安心感，确保一切"正常"。
Scenario: Good or bad, people enjoy checking their cameras for a variety of reasons. Security is not the only reason people have cameras.
情景：无论好坏，人们出于各种原因喜欢查看他们的摄像头。安全不是人们拥有摄像头的唯一原因。

Window Films, Privacy Screens, Curtains

窗膜、隐私屏幕、窗帘

Reinforced glass (prevents smashes), bars prevent entry and curtains block inside view. People may not want others seeing inside their homes while they are home or away. There might be something valuable, private, or the owner just wants extra privacy.

加固玻璃（防止砸碎）、栅栏防止进入，窗帘阻挡内部视线。人们可能不希望其他人在他们在家或外出时看到他们家里的情况。可能有贵重物品、私人物品，或者业主只是想要额外的隐私。
Scenario: TVs, computers, photos, electronics, furniture, game systems, and anything else of value can sometimes be seen easily through a window. At night, if you are outside, it is easy to see inside a ligthed room but hard to see outside from a lighted room. Most homes have a "ground floor".
情景：电视、电脑、照片、电子设备、家具、游戏系统和其他任何有价值的东西有时可以通过窗户轻易看到。晚上，如果您在外面，很容易看到亮着灯的房间内部，但从亮着灯的房间里很难看到外面。大多数房屋都有"一楼"。

Reinforced Doors & Windows

加固门窗

Heavy-duty deadbolts, strike plates, reinforced doors, bars on windows, extram locks, and shatter-resistant glass stop forced entry.

重型防盗锁、撞击板、加固门、窗户栅栏、额外锁具和抗碎玻璃阻止强行进入。
Scenario: A home tends to have multiple entry points, even through the garage. Most owners know their homes weakest points and will attempt to secure them in some way. This is especially prevelant if they encountered any previous crimes.
情景：房屋往往有多个入口点，甚至通过车库。大多数业主知道他们家最薄弱的地方，并会尝试以某种方式保护它们。如果他们以前遇到过任何犯罪，这种情况尤其普遍。

Interior – Active Response & Containment

室内 - 主动响应和遏制

Alarm systems, smart locks, and final defensive measures

警报系统、智能锁和最终防御措施

Alarm Systems

警报系统

Sensors and sirens alert owners and authorities to intrusions or unusual activity.

传感器和警报器向业主和当局警报入侵或异常活动。
Scenario: Setting an alarm while away or at home alone is very common with the people who own these systems. There might be a special code involved that is secretly shared with friends and family. Rooms that have higher priced valuables or family tend to be prioritized with sensors.
情景：外出或独自在家时设置警报对于拥有这些系统的人来说非常常见。可能涉及一个秘密与朋友和家人分享的特殊代码。有较高价值物品或家人的房间往往优先安装传感器。

Locks & Smart Locks

锁具和智能锁

Bedrooms and bathrooms may all have locks on the doors in a house, usually for privacy and may be locked at night or while home alone. Main doors tend to have heavier duty locks than other internal doors. Keyless systems with PIN codes, biometrics, or app control prevent lock tampering.

房屋中的卧室和浴室可能都在门上有锁，通常是为了隐私，可能在夜间或独自在家时锁上。主门往往比其他内部门有更重的锁。带PIN码、生物识别或应用控制的无钥匙系统防止锁具篡改。
Scenario: Somebody might mess with a keyless lock and you could recieve notifications. In the event of a break-ins looks on bathroom and bedroom doors could be useful.
情景：有人可能会弄乱无钥匙锁，您可能会收到通知。在入室盗窃事件中，浴室和卧室门上的锁可能很有用。

Interior Cameras

室内摄像机

Indoor cams set up to watch certain rooms, pets, kids, elders and potential intrusions.

室内摄像机设置用于监视特定房间、宠物、孩子、老人和潜在的入侵行为。
Scenario: Someone enters my home while I am away, I can see who they are and what they do. Being gone for an extended period of times can cause anxiety, checking inside your home can bring added relief. Possible to also watch items or speicifc activities.
情景：当我不在家时有人进入我家，我可以看到他们是谁以及他们做什么。长时间外出可能会引起焦虑，检查家里的情况可以带来额外的安心感。也可能监视物品或特定活动。

Vantage Points & Weapons

制高点和武器

Homes with 2 stories offer an unique advantage from the upstairs. Most people will break in on the ground floor, so sleeping and being upstairs gives a homeowner a funneled vantage point in the event of an intrustion.

两层楼的房屋在楼上提供了独特的优势。大多数人会从一楼闯入，所以睡觉和在楼上给房主在入侵事件中提供了一个有利的制高点。

It is not uncommon for non-gun homeowners to keep a weapon like a knife or bat next to their beds if they have to approach an instrusion.

对于非枪支拥有者的房主来说，如果他们必须面对入侵，在床边放置刀或球棒等武器并不少见。
Scenario: At home, in bed, in the dark can be a high anxiety drive for people, IF a noise is heard the reaction may include grabbing a weapon, being quiet, and locking your bedroom door.
情景：在家里、在床上、在黑暗中对人们来说可能是高度焦虑的，如果听到噪音，反应可能包括抓取武器、保持安静和锁上卧室门。

Firearms (in gun-owning households)

枪支（在拥有枪支的家庭中）

Used as a final defensive option where legal and trained.

在合法和受过训练的情况下作为最终防御选项使用。
Scenario: A homeowner calls 911 and secures their family while armed until help arrives. Will use if necessary.
情景：房主拨打911并在武装状态下保护家人直到帮助到达。必要时会使用。
